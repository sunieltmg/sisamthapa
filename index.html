Grandy International Hospital

Grande International Hospital, Kathmandu is a multi-specialty hospital that offers preventive and curative healthcare services. It was established with a motive “Care to Cure” in February 2010.

SERVICES

Emergency Services

State-of-the-art 15 bed Emergency Room with a resuscitation room and a stand-by trauma team. 24hr consultant on stand-by.

Rapid Rescue

A 24x7 standby emergency medical team is ready for rescue from any part of Nepal, which includes a trauma consultant and paramedics with all equipment.

Assisted Fertility Center

Grande International Hospital’s Assisted Fertility Center is a dedicated department that has been setup to help parents who have not been able to conceive the natural way.
